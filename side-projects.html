My Side Projects

Since 2016, I have embarked on numerous side projects spanning various domains of software development and technology. Each project has been an opportunity to explore new technologies, address unique challenges, and contribute to open-source communities. Although I’ve worked on many projects over the years, I’ve compiled a selection of notable ones below. These represent a mix of tools, applications, and databases that I’ve developed or contributed to:

  • UDOM Timetable App - A Flutter application for managing and viewing timetables for students at the University of Dodoma. The app integrates with Firebase and a custom backend to provide real-time updates.
  • NECTA Database - A comprehensive database solution designed for the National Examinations Council of Tanzania, facilitating efficient management and access to examination data.
  • 10 Fast Fingers Tool - A tool developed to analyze and enhance typing speed on the 10 Fast Fingers platform, including features to improve typing accuracy and speed.
  • UDOM RATIBA API - An API service providing access to timetable data for the University of Dodoma, designed to support various student and administrative applications.
  • Cafeterion App - A customer management application for Cafeterion, aimed at streamlining customer interactions, orders, and feedback management.

These projects highlight my ongoing commitment to exploring new technologies and solving real-world problems through innovative solutions. If you're interested in any of these projects or would like to collaborate on future endeavors, feel free to reach out!
